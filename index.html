Hi and welcome to my website!

My name is Sivani and I am an environmental chemist. I completed my PhD at the University of Toronto in the summer of 2022. Now, I am working as a post-doctoral researcher with ZeroPM at the Norwegian Geotechnical Institute in Oslo. My current research uses environmental chemistry and cheminformatics to identify persistent and mobile substances on the global market.

Here you will find information about my research interests, writing and publications, and some of my passion projects, including my work in science policy.

Feel free to email me if you have any questions, are looking for PDF copies of my publications, or wish to collaborate.

You can access the KOA database here. If you have any comments or publications you wish to see added to the database let me know using this form.
