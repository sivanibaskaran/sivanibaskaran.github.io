Hi and welcome to my website!

My name is Sivani and I am an environmental chemist, 2023-2024 NSERC Postdoctoral Fellow, 2023 CAS Future Leader and science policy enthusiast. I completed my PhD at the University of Toronto in the summer of 2022. Now, I a post-doctoral researcher with ZeroPM at the Norwegian Geotechnical Institute in Oslo. My research uses environmental chemistry and cheminformatics to identify persistent and mobile substances on the global market. Right now, I am exploring how Bayesian Analysis approaches can be used to determine a consensus value for organic-carbon - water partition ratios (KOC) in mobility hazard assessments.

Here you will find information about my research interests, writing and publications, and some of my passion projects, including my work in science policy.

Feel free to email me if you have any questions, are looking for PDF copies of my publications, or wish to collaborate.
