I offer one-on-one tutoring in science and chemistry for secondary school and university students. My teaching emphasizes conceptual clarity, quantitative reasoning, and structured problem-solving.


## High School

### Science (up to Grade 10)
- Scientific reasoning and problem-solving
- Test preparation

### Chemistry (Grades 11–12)
- Acids and bases
- Chemical equilibrium
- Electrochemistry
- Organic chemistry
- Reaction mechanisms
- Stoichiometry
- Thermochemistry

## University Level

- General Chemistry
- Physical Chemistry
- Environmental Chemistry
- Support for lab reports and assignments
- Guidance on modelling and data interpretation

## Teaching Experience

I have extensive experience teaching undergraduate chemistry courses at the University of Toronto, including introductory chemistry, physical and environmental chemistry. My background includes laboratory instruction, tutorial delivery, grading, and guest lecturing.
